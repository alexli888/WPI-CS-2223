1. “Double Trouble” is really a particular instantiation of the fundamental combinatorial game.
    What is the real name of the game, who “solved” it, and
    when? Where does it show up in popular culture? [2 bonus points]

Answer:
    Double trouble is really a particular instantiation of the Nim game.
    The game of Nim was first "solved" by Charles L. Bouton in 1901.
    The game of Nim shows up in Books, Movies, and Video Games.
